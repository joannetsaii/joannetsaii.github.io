Overview

During the summer of 2022, I had the opportunity to be a part of Google's Nest Software Platform Team. The team works on building more general features incorporated into multiple Nest products including cameras, doorbells and Chromecast.

Projects

The project I was responsible for focuses on the functionality of live-view video conversion and display on an internal developer tool. In this refactor plan, I replaced the original approach, which is to call by system command, by integrating an open source library API. Before diving into the program, I did a thorough research on the third party library as well as the relevant codebases, organized documents of notes, and reported to my hosts during meetings.

To maximize my time at Google, I continued on to learn test writing skills and styles at Google, and worked on unit tests for the refactored code.

Life @ Google

Besides project, I volunteered as an "Intern Champion" to help the Intern Team hold offsite social events for the interns, intending to boost community engagement and bring everyone together. As one of the organizers, I had the chance to interact with more people directly by making announcements in the group and welcoming everyone at the events, and I truly enjoyed the process.

I am also glad to maintain a good work life balance here. Through playing foosball and chess, which my hosts are really good at, I got to build a tighter connection with not only other interns but my hosts and other full-time employees on the Nest team. We spent our lunch break and Friday afternoons taking breaks from work and bonding, which created a close community - what an honor to be involved in!

Finally, a huge thank you to the whole Nest Software team, especially my host, Jhih-Ming Huang, and my co-host, Dai-Yang Wu. I am grateful to work with and have the support of these talented individuals. I look forward to applying the skills I learned this summer to my future work!
